Financial Technology

Financial technology is the new technology and innovation that aims to compete with traditional financial methods in the delivery of financial services. It is a new industry that uses technology to improve activities in finance.

ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE IN THE FINANCIAL SECTOR
1. Advanced customer service

Robo-advisors. Robo-advisors use algorithms to give advice on financial investments. Using robo-advisors, investors can check how high is the risk if they invest money in that or another deal.

2. Fraud detection

An AI-based fraud prediction system can learn millions of possible fraud attacks, memorize them and analyze threats.

3. Lowering the risk in P2P lending

P2P lending organizations can also benefit from AI since they can analyze the behavior of customer and detect all potential risks in cooperation with a certain customer.

4. Improved insurance support

Helps automates hedge funds(an investment fund that pools capital from accredited individuals or institutional investors and invests in a variety of assets, often with complex portfolio-construction and risk-management techniques), making the process more secure and precise.

5. Enhanced investment analysis

AI can help investors look for good sources to invest their money.
