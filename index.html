Questions?
  • What is FinTech?
    Financial Technology, or FinTech, refers to the technology-enabled innovation in financial services. It involves spurring new business models, applications, and processes such as payment applications and platforms in mobile devices and computers. FinTech, as of now, is having a transformative effect towards financial markets,institutions, and on the provision of financial services as a whole.
  • How Did FinTech Come About?
    The financial crisis that occured of 2008 gave an opening for people to introduce a new model in alternative financial services which eventually gave rise to the FinTech industry.
  • What is the Driving Force behind FinTech?
    The Generation Y Also known as the Millennial Generation, is a generation shaped by technology and openness to social media. This generation felt that the traditional model does not really suite them in managing their finances. This led rise to the application of Technology (Artificial Intelligence) into Finance which was later coined as “FinTech”.
    Technological innovation fuelled by a more connected society, availability of information and the ability to acquire and analyse big data. This is not only fuelling alternative finance start-ups, but helping traditional players deliver better customer insight and experience
